Task 2: Browser-based javaScript tool

Goal: Demonstrate your ability to design and write web-based applications.

Create a browser-based JavaScript tool for interactive visualizations of mutations in cancer. Your tool will retrieve data from an API provided by the Data Coordination Center of the International Cancer Genomics Consortium.

What have I done?

First, I tried to analyze my given data. Therefore I downloaded the given URL and converted the JSON File in a CSV. I realized that 2 out of 22 huma autosome paris, namely Chromosome 8 and Chromosome 18, are missing. Additionally, one out of two sex chromosomes,namely Chromosome Y, is missing as well. Without displaying them in the further charts, I created two simple bar charts and added interacions and filters. Therefore, I came in touch with the given dataset. After adding crossfilter with brushing and linking interaction techniques, I additionally created a parallel coordinates plot with two related tables. The provided interaction techniques comprises now highlighting, filtering as well as selecting and searching specific mutation types / mutation IDs.
